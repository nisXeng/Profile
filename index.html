01 — About

Part engineer,
part storyteller.

A short note on how I think, what I obsess over, and why details matter more than features.

I spend my days translating abstract ideas into tactile, kinetic interfaces — the kind that feel less like software and more like a moment you remember.

My background is half visual design, half front-end engineering. That blend means I sketch in Figma in the morning and tune GLSL fragment shaders by the afternoon — and I think that's where the most interesting work lives.

"Good design whispers. Great design makes you forget the screen is there."

When I'm not in the editor, you'll find me running long distances badly, restoring vintage typewriters, or chasing perfect espresso in cafés I haven't been to yet.
